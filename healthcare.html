Healthcare

With the pandemic putting healthcare front and center, both old-guard and new biotech companies have stole the show. As new technologies like mRNA are setting the standard for future vaccines, pipelines are looking more and more dominant with the "partnership" style of the industry. Although in the sector-weight section of our portfolio, we are looking to put more money to work in the second-half of the year due to the defensive nature of the space, a perfect counter to what should be an extremly choppy market as we close out 2021.
