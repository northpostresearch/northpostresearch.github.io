Industrials

With Biden's trillion-dollar infrastructure deal passing in November, industrials look like an extremely attractive investment opportunity. However, the spread of the delta, omicron, and other future variants is throwing a wrench into these plans. Combining this headwind with high inflation and labor costs, we have been forced to turn cautiously bullish on the future despite the clear positive from lawmakers.
