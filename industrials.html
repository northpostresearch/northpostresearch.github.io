A number of infrastructure legislations have created structural spending tailwinds focusing on supply chain improvement and reshoring. Many firms are trying to align themselves with greener long-term trends, including net-zero construction, mineral and aggregate offerings, and transforming end-markets for their portfolio of products. Both provide a number of growth opportunities that go against the current grain of slow economic growth in 2023.
