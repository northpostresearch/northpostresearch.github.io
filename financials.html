Financials

With a fed funds rate expected to be above 3% till 2025 and higher than any rate over the past decade, financials, especially banks, have a long runway of net interest margin growth. Rising rates should also allow companies to invest client cash in high-yield instruments, improving cash flow generation and balance sheets, and creating a bullish environment for financials in H1 2023.
