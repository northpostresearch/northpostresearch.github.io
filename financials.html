Financials

With the Fed gearing up to taper and our optimistic outlook that the 10-Year Treasury rate will push upwards of 2.00%, financials are the place to be. Additionally, we are seeing the strongest ever spending from the American consumer, with credit card delinquency rates at an all-time low. While investors should be alert for variant-related economic setbacks, the sector is set up for long-term success.
