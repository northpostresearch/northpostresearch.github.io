Financials

Banks will face the regulatory fallout of liquidity issues that forced the closure and consolidation of specialized and regional banks. While investment banks and capital-focused services will face additional headwinds from a higher-for-longer fed funds rate of 5%+, there are winners in specialty insurance, which is seeing improvement in demand, allowing them the flexibility to grow their balance sheet by maintaining a risk-on investment strategy while also investing client cash into high-yield instruments.
