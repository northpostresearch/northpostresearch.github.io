Semiconductors

As the chip supply glut is nearing an end, the industry is about to enter one of its most bullish cycles in history. Spending on generative AI, IoT, SiC, and many more tailwinds is reverberating throughout the industry, from chip design to equipment providers. Even with the US-China tech war, as both commerce departments banning exports of advanced semiconductors, domestic firms that improved their supply chains will see strong demand in an environment where the US wants to be number one.
