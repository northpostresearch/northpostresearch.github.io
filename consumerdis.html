Consumer Discrentionary

The three-headed monster of the supply chain issues, delta and omicron variants, and inflationary issues are making life tough for companies. Not to mention the fact the holiday season showed us that there is a spending discrepancy in the country, with middle and lower class spending less and the rich spending more - not a recipe for sustained success. As the headwinds continue to pile up, we plan to avoid the sector until a clearer outlook emerges.
