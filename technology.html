Sustainability Highlights

  • Facilitated access to over $24.2B in capital for small and medium-sized businesses since 2013, with $8.2B in the pandemic, providing a lifeline and supporting growth for many mom-and-pop retailers
  • Diverse leadership committee where 56% of executives are women and/or POCs, while on a company scale, they have achieved 100% intersectional pay equity
  • An internal security program is certified through the federally compatible ISO 27001 program, leading to a record low transaction loss rate of 0.09%
