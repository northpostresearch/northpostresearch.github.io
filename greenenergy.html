Green Energy

The Inflation Reduction Act laid the foundations for green energy spending with various types of manufacturing and investment tax credits, however, the sector took this news too far. Bubbled-up valuations and spending despite poor balance sheets aren't a great mix considering the current economic backdrop and shouldn't attract much capital in H1 2023.
