Green Energy

With the increased emphasis on creating a better planet, green companies are the future, however, much of it became baked into stock price during 2020 presidential election cycle. Although we love the goals these companies look to achieve, the run-up in prices has forced us to avoid the area and come back when prices are at more attractive levels.

Sustainability Highlights

  • Doubled the average solar module wattage from 278W to upwards of 500W while creating a more sustainable solar manufacturing process by reducing water intensity by 44% with goals of reducing it by another 49% by 2025 given a 2020 baseline
  • End-of-life solar recycling initiative in Australia alongside Reclaim PV Rcycling which has sent over 6,000 solar modules for recycling and end-of-life management
  • Published a supplier code of conduct to assess suppliers' ESG policies and set strict human rights, conflict minerals, environmental protection, health and safety, and business ethics guidlines
  • Founded the Candian Solar University program which provides entry level (101) to expert level (401) courses to employees of all background in order to accelerate project development, enhance R&D, and promote more educated employees at all levels
  • With 35% female-led workforce, 22% of which are in management positions including the Chief Sustainability Officer, they had founded two gender equality initiatives called 'WIRE: Women in Renewable Energy' and 'WISE: Women in Solar Energy'
  • Final Scores - Environmental (9/10), Social (10/10), Governance (8/10)
